Coming to La Boutardière

La Boutardière is 2.5 hours by car from Paris, 6.5 hours by car from Marseille, 1 hour by car from Bourges, 30 minutes by car from Vierzon, 20 minutes by car from Châteauroux, 15 minutes by car from Issoudun, 12 minutes by car from La Greletterie. 1 km from the A20 freeway, exit 11.

By train, the nearest and most accessible stations are Vierzon, Châteauroux and Issoudun.
